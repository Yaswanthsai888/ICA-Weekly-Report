The Problem 😅

Meet The Old Way™

A beautiful manual process that nobody asked for but everyone inherited. Starring: Excel, a patient human, and a Teams chat nobody wanted to be tagged in.

📋
Step 1 — The Tracker
Every team member fills an Excel tracker after using ICA. Simple enough… unless you forget. Which people do. Constantly. Every. Single. Day.
🕵️
Step 2 — Enter: Yaswanth
He manually checks the tracker, hunts down names, writes them on a notepad (yes, an actual physical notepad in 2024 😂), and prepares "The Shame List".
💬
Step 3 — The Announcement
Posts in the Teams group chat — everyone sees it, mild panic ensues, people scramble to update. Zero analytics. Zero insights. Maximum awkward silence. 😬
The Infamous Message 😬

Problems Identified 🔍

Why This Didn't Work at All

😩 The Painful Reality

  • ⏰Yaswanth checks the tracker manually every single day
  • 📝Handwritten notes — error-prone, no backup, easily lost
  • 😤Way too much human effort for a repetitive task
  • 📊Zero analytics — no trends, no patterns, no insights at all
  • 🤷No idea who consistently misses vs first-time offenders
  • 📅No history — what happened last month? ¯\_(ツ)_/¯
  • 🔁Same reminder every day, forever — completely soul-crushing

🤔 What We Actually Needed

  • 🤖Automation — let a machine handle the boring stuff
  • 📈Real analytics — usage trends, patterns, team insights
  • 🎯Smart reminders — right person, right time, automatically
  • 📊Monthly and weekly dashboards — data at a glance
  • 🔔Teams notifications that send themselves
  • 👀Visibility into who uses which ICA tool and when
  • 🧠A system that scales and doesn't depend on one person
